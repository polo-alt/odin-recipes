DESCRIPTION

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Our homemade lasagna recipe is sure to bring delight!

And this is me making this up as we go along

INGREDIENTS

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb italian beef sausage
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1lb of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a non stick pan, sweat onions over medium heat. Add crushed crushed garlic and ground beef until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Season with your favorite seasoning. Let it come to a boil then reduce heat to simmer for about 1-1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. While preheating, combine ricotta cheese with egg, 1/4 cup parmesan, and salt to taste.
  4. To assemble: spread meat sauce at the bottom of a square baking dish. Layer lasagna sheets over the meat sauce, enough to cover. Sread ricotta mixture then place mozarella slices on top. Spoon meat sauce over the ricotta and cheese slices, sprinkle with grated parmesan. Repeat layers and finish off the top with remaining mozza and parm cheeses.
  5. Cover dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Serve warm or cool.
